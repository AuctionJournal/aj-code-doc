## Live Lot

- Before auction open bidding
  - Bidding not open yet show start bid, quantity and time open bidding
- After auction open bidding
  - For all auction except onsite with live webcast auction and onsite with live webcast auction with prebidding
  - Bidder can place bid
  - Show current bid , bid count, bidding open
- Live webcast live lot
  - For onsite with live webcast auction, live lot in any ring of that auction
  - show current bid , bid count , lot is live
  - Give bid live button to go to live ring
- Soft close lot
  - For all auction except onsite with live webcast auction , ---
- Closed lot
  - In onsite with live webcast auction, lot will be closed when clerked.
  - In all other auction lot will be closed one by one.
  - Each lot will have independent close time.
